Welcome to the Scraper Blogs Project!

Following are the details about the project:

  • There are around 60 blogs included.
  • Each blog contains numerous articles
  • For each article, following attributes will be scraped:
    • Title of the article
    • Total word count in the article
    • Article published date
    • Article author
    • Total comments count in the article
  • Each scraped data can be viewed or downloaded as a csv file
